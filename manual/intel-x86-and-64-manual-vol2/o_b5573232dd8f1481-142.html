3.1.1.11   Flags Affected Section 

The ‚ÄúFlags Affected‚Äù section lists the flags in the EFLAGS register that are affected by the instruction. When a flag 
is cleared, it is equal to 0; when it is set, it is equal to 1. The arithmetic and logical instructions usually assign values 
to the status flags in a uniform manner (see Appendix A, ‚ÄúEFLAGS Cross-Reference,‚Äù in the Intel¬Æ 64 and IA-32 
Architectures Software Developer‚Äôs Manual, Volume 1). Non-conventional assignments are described in the ‚ÄúOper-
ation‚Äù section. The values of flags listed as undefined may be changed by the instruction in an indeterminate 
manner. Flags that are not listed are unchanged by the instruction.

3.1.1.12   FPU Flags Affected Section 

The floating-point instructions have an ‚ÄúFPU Flags Affected‚Äù section that describes how each instruction can affect 
the four condition code flags of the FPU status word.
